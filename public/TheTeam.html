Meet the people behind our success

Thato Rampedi
Brand Strategist
Thato Rampedi is a distinguished brand strategist and content creator who embarked on this career path in 2018. Recognized for his inventive approach, he has diligently built his personal brand. Thato has managed to carve a unique space for himself in the digital world through vlogging and collaborations with his close friends, showing his ability to leverage personal relationships and digital platforms to maximize his brand's impact.

In a testament to his versatile digital skills, Thato has expanded his content creation to include podcasting, where he co-hosts a show with his brother. This move not only highlights his adaptability but also showcases his ability to capitalize on various content formats to engage his audience.

Thato's business acumen is evident in his entrepreneurial ventures. He is currently applying his branding experience and business knowledge in new ways by managing another content creator, Mammi Dearest. This venture is an exhibition of his ability to adapt his skills to diverse scenarios and manage brands other than his own, highlighting his strategic and managerial capabilities as well as a newly launched podcast under his channel called Thought Digest, a podcast that delves into womanhood and the everything surrounding woman.

As a member of the YouTube group As Majita, Thato continues to push the boundaries of content creation. The group focuses on producing creative and adventurous content, allowing Thato to further expand his portfolio and reach. His collaborations with brands such as Xiaomi, LoversPlus, and MrD delivery demonstrate his knack for brand strategy and his ability to create content that resonates with audiences, attracting high-profile partnerships.

Thato's skills as a producer shine through in his work on the dating show, Finding Mjolo. Over several seasons, he has partnered with various brands to create unique themes and aesthetics for the show. In season four, he worked with Lovers Plus to bring messages of diversity and sensuality to the forefront. The third season, in partnership with Mr D Delivery, featured a summer aesthetic that celebrated various foods on different dates, showcasing his ability to incorporate brand partnerships into the show's format creatively.

However, Thato's most significant accomplishment to date is the production of AreYouTubedIn Season one, a digital reality TV show filmed in Cape Town. The show brought together multiple content creators for a drama-filled weekend, and it quickly became the number one trending show at the time of its release. This achievement solidified Thato Rampedi's reputation as a formidable producer in the digital space and a leading figure in content creation and brand strategy.
Mbali Gama
Creative Content Director

Mbali Gama is a highly experienced Creative Director at Rampedi Media, a position she has thrived in for nearly three years. Working hand in hand with Thato Rampedi, she brings her unique creative vision to life across all the company's productions.

She has been instrumental in directing three seasons of the popular show 'Looking for Mjolo'. This role allowed her to extensively refine her skills in both directing and production design, underscoring her commitment to excellence and eye for detail. Her ability to harmonize various creative elements and lead a diverse team towards a shared vision has been central to her success.

Her skills in directing were further emphasized in the company's flagship production show, 'AreYouTubedIn'. This show is a testament to Gama's ability to push boundaries and innovate within her field. With this production, she and the crew of the production have broken new ground in South Africa's digital production landscape, highlighting the potential and vast possibilities of digital productions.

Her role within the company has evolved over time as she now holds the position of Creative Content Director. In this capacity, she works closely with Thato Rampedi, assisting him in shaping the brand and creative direction across all his business ventures. Her influence can be seen in every aspect of the company's creative output, from the subtle nuances in the brand's visual identity to the overarching narratives in their productions.

In conclusion, Mbali Gama's role at Rampedi Media is multifaceted and dynamic. Her commitment to her craft, combined with her ability to innovate and adapt, has made her an invaluable asset to the company and a key figure in the South African digital production landscape.
